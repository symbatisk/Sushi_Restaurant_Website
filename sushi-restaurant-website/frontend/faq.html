How can we help you?

Learn more about our service and helpful tips for ordering sush

The delivery time for sushi orders depends on the location and the current workload of the restaurant. Usually, we aim to deliver orders within 30-60 minutes after they are placed. However, during peak hours or holidays, delivery times may be slightly longer. We strive to process all orders promptly and deliver them as quickly as possible while maintaining the quality of the dishes.

Our menu features a wide selection of sushi, including classic rolls, nigiri, sashimi, sets, and specialty rolls. We also offer vegetarian and vegan sushi options to cater to the needs of all our customers.

We regularly run various promotions and offers for our customers. These may include discounts on specific types of sushi, gifts with orders, or special combo deals. To stay informed about all our promotions, you can subscribe to our newsletter or follow us on social media.

Information about the ingredients of each roll or dish is available on our website or in the ordering app. You can find a complete description of the ingredients and information about possible allergens. If you have any special requirements regarding the ingredients of dishes, please contact our operator when placing your order.

We offer various payment methods for ordering, including online payment by credit card through our secure payment system, cash payment upon delivery, or payment via electronic wallets. When placing an order, you can choose the most convenient payment option for you.
